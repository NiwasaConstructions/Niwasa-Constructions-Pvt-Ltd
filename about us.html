About Niwasa Constructions

Building Excellence Since Day One

Niwasa Constructions (Pvt) Ltd was founded with a vision to elevate the standard of construction in Sri Lanka. We blend traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge architectural design to create spaces that are durable and beautiful.

Our expert engineers and architects work closely with clients to ensure every luxury finish exceeds expectations.

Niwasa Construction (Pvt) Ltd is a premier general contracting firm based at No. 03 Bowala Lane, Kandy, specializing in the design and execution of residential and small-scale commercial projects. Under the leadership of Managing Director Janaka Bandara Aththanayaka, our company has built a strong reputation for combining traditional Vastu Vidya principles with modern architectural innovation. Our expert team, including esteemed professionals like Architect Sujeewa Herath and Structural Engineer Lakruwan Fernando, is uniquely experienced in handling challenging terrains, such as hillside construction projects in areas like Talathuoya.With over 100,000 followers on our official Facebook page, we maintain transparency and trust by showcasing a diverse portfolio of ongoing and completed works, including major projects in Gannoruwa, Polgolla, Waligalla, and Theldeniya. Our comprehensive service suite covers everything from house planning and 3D modeling to detailed B.O.Q. estimates and high-end finishes like modern timber flooring. We are dedicated to providing a "stress-free" construction experience, assisting our clients with essential local authority approvals and ensuring every project is guided by rigorous engineering supervision. Recognized with a 5-star rating for professional craftsmanship, Niwasa Construction remains a trusted partner in turning your architectural dreams into enduring realities.
