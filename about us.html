Niwasa Construction (Pvt) Ltd is a premier general contracting firm based in Kandy, specializing in the design and execution of residential and small-scale commercial projects.

We have built a strong reputation for combining traditional Vastu Vidya principles with modern architectural innovation. We are dedicated to providing a "stress-free" construction experience, assisting our clients with essential local authority approvals and ensuring every project is guided by rigorous engineering supervision.

  • Expert Team: Guided by esteemed professionals like Architect Sujeewa Herath and Structural Engineer Lakruwan Fernando.
  • Challenging Terrains: Uniquely experienced in handling hillside construction projects in areas like Talathuoya.
  • Transparency & Trust: Showcasing a diverse portfolio of major projects in Gannoruwa, Polgolla, Waligalla, and Theldeniya.
  • Comprehensive Service: From 3D modeling and BOQ estimates to high-end finishes like modern timber flooring.
